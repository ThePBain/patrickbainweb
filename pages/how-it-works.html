How it works

The process is designed to keep scope clear and delivery predictable. You will always know what stage the project is in and what comes next.

1

Discovery

We clarify your goal, your offer, and what customers need to do on the site.

2

Build

I design and develop the site with clear checkpoints and fast feedback loops.

3

Launch

We finalize content, test everything, and launch with support options available.

What I need from you

  • Business basics (services, location, hours)
  • Any existing logo or brand colors (optional)
  • Photos you want to use (or I can source placeholders)
  • Primary goal (calls, leads, booking, payments, credibility)
  • Any reference websites you like (optional)

What you can expect

  • Clear scope and timeline confirmation before work begins
  • A clean, modern layout aligned to your business
  • Mobile-first design and fast load times
  • Clear calls to action and contact flow
  • Launch support and optional ongoing maintenance
