Service Packages

Packages are designed around business outcomes, not technical implementation. Pricing is confirmed before work begins, and scope stays clear throughout the project.

Standard deliverables

Included in every package

  • Mobile-first, responsive design
  • Clean visual system and consistent layout
  • Basic on-page SEO (page titles and meta descriptions)
  • Analytics installed (client provides necessary access)
  • SSL enabled (hosting dependent)
  • Basic performance optimization
  • Launch support

Revisions

Each package includes one revision round for reasonable changes such as text edits, image swaps, and small layout refinements. Major redesigns, new pages, or new features are quoted separately.

Payment terms

Standard terms are 50% to begin work and 50% due before launch. Smaller projects may be paid in full upfront.

What is not included unless quoted

  • Professional photography or videography
  • Logo design or full brand identity work
  • Large-scale copywriting
  • Advanced SEO campaigns
  • Ongoing edits after launch outside a maintenance plan
  • Paid tools or subscriptions required for specific features (client pays directly)
