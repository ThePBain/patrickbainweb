Maintenance Plans

Websites are not finished at launch. Updates, monitoring, and small changes are expected over time. Maintenance keeps your site secure, reliable, and up to date so you do not have to manage the technical details.

Essential Care

Stability and security
$50 / month

For clients who want their website kept stable and secure with minimal involvement.

  • Software updates (if applicable)
  • Regular backups
  • Basic security monitoring
  • Uptime monitoring
  • Small text or image changes (limited)
  • General technical support

Growth Care

Most common choice
$100 / month

For clients who want ongoing support and light optimization.

  • Everything in Essential Care
  • Performance checks
  • Basic analytics review
  • Priority support
  • Monthly small improvements or adjustments
  • Minor layout or content refinements

Premium Care

Continuous improvement
$200 / month

For clients who want proactive optimization and growth-focused updates.

  • Everything in Growth Care
  • Conversion-focused improvements
  • SEO adjustments and recommendations
  • Monthly performance and analytics summary
  • Proactive feature suggestions
  • Highest priority support

Definition of small changes

Small changes typically include:

  • Text edits
  • Replacing images
  • Updating hours, phone numbers, or links
  • Minor layout adjustments within an existing section

Scope and boundaries

Maintenance does not include major redesigns, new pages, or new feature builds. Larger requests are quoted separately. Response time and scope depend on the plan level.

Maintenance can be canceled with notice, but is recommended for long-term reliability.
