Terms of Service

These terms outline general expectations when working with Patrick Bain Web Services.

Quotes and scope

All projects are quoted before work begins. Scope, timeline, and deliverables are confirmed in writing. Work outside the agreed scope may require a revised quote.

Payments

Standard terms are 50% to begin work and 50% due before launch, unless otherwise agreed.

Revisions

Packages include one revision round for reasonable changes such as text edits, image swaps, and small layout refinements. Major redesigns or new features are quoted separately.

Third-party services

Some features may require third-party services (hosting, booking tools, payment processors). Any paid subscriptions required for the client’s site are typically paid by the client directly.

Maintenance

Maintenance plans provide ongoing support. Large changes or new features are not included unless quoted.

Contact

Questions about these terms can be sent to contact@patrickbainweb.com.
